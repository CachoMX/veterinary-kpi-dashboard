GA4 KPI Dashboard Help

üìä Tracked Metrics

  • Key Events: Number of important user actions tracked
  • New Users: First-time visitors to your domain
  • Active Users: Users who engaged with your site
  • Engagement Rate: Percentage of engaged sessions
  • Avg Engagement Time: Average time users spend engaged (in seconds)

üéØ Benchmark Criteria

For each metric, domains pass if their trend is steady or increasing (‚Üë or ‚Üí) compared to the same month last year.

The 90% threshold means at least 90% of domains must show steady/increasing trends for a metric to pass.

üîÑ Syncing Data

  1. Select the month you want to analyze
  2. Click "Sync from GA4" to fetch fresh data from Google Analytics
  3. Data is cached for 24 hours to reduce API calls
  4. Click "Load Data" to view cached metrics without re-syncing
